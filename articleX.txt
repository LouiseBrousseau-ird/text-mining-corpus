## Article formatting for automated text-mining (kewords and keyexpressions extraction). 
## Encoding : ANSI - latin 1
\\YEAR
2010 # the publication year of the article
\\TITLE
This is a title # paste the title (single line)
\\ABSTRACT
This si the abstract # paste the abstract (single line)
\\KEYWORDS
keword1 keyword2, keyword3, etc # paste article keywords (single line, coma-separated, "NA" if no keywords)
\\TEXT
# text of the article (mutli-lines) 
# Must include :
# introduction + M&M + Results + DIscussion + Conclusion
# Must exclude :
# figures + bibliography + aknowledgements + supporting information etc.
